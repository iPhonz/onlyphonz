About

Alphonzo Terrell is a dynamic entrepreneur and the driving force behind SPILL, a conversational platform recognized for its innovation in social networking and community engagement. With a passion for building inclusive digital experiences, he has led SPILL to critical acclaim and sustainable growth.

  • Featured as Apple's App of the Day in 2024.
  • Named one of Fast Company's Most Innovative Companies of 2024.
  • Highlighted on the Google Play Store homepage for exceptional user engagement.
  • Developed and launched the equity subscription model, pioneering new revenue streams for social platforms.
  • Led a successful crowdfunding campaign, raising $500K from community investors.
